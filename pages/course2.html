What is the significance of ISO 26262 in Functional Safety?

ISO 26262 is an international standard ensuring the safety of automotive E/E systems, defining risk assessment and mitigation strategies for road vehicles.

The course provides foundational knowledge for ISO 26262 certification. Completion of this training will help you qualify for certification exams.
